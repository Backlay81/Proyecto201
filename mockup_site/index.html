About This Project

This tool is developed for internal use to improve our content creation process for websites and YouTube channels. By leveraging the Google Ads API, we can make data-driven decisions about which topics to create content for.

The Keyword Research Automation Tool helps us identify profitable niches and optimize our content for search engines, resulting in better visibility and engagement.

This is a private tool used only by our team and is not available for public use or as a service to third parties.
